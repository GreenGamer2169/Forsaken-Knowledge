Health and stamina are the 2 bars displayed on the bottom right of your screen. Health is the red bar and stamina is the white bar.

Basic tips: NEVER let your stamina reach 0 EVER not as killer or survivor.

Survivors have 100 stamina they lose 10 stamina per second while sprinting and gain 20 stamina per second while not sprinting or standing still.

Killers have 110 stamina they lose 9 stamina per second while sprinting and gain 21 stamina per second while not sprinting ot standing still.

Killer have more stamina than survivors lose it slower and gain it faster. killers are faster sprinting than survivors but slower when walking. If you get to 0 stamina the run icon will turn red once the red goes away you can regain stamina. but will regain it 20% slower untill you reach 36 stamina.
